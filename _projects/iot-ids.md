Two related publications on applying machine learning to intrusion detection in constrained network environments.

**IoT IDS (ICTIST 2022).** Designed and evaluated a lightweight intrusion detection system for IoT infrastructure, focusing on detection accuracy under the resource constraints typical of edge devices. Presented at the 1st International Conference on Trends and Innovations in Smart Technologies, London, UK.

**Smart Cities IDS (EAI Transactions, 2023).** Extended the approach to Smart Cities networks, incorporating ensemble ML methods to improve detection rates across heterogeneous traffic profiles. Published in *EAI Endorsed Transactions on Smart Cities*, Vol. 7, No. 1.

**Stack.** Python · scikit-learn · network traffic datasets · feature engineering pipelines
